Arduino & ESP32 Bluetooth Controller App - Dabble

❮ ❯
Whether you're a student, a teacher, or a hobbyist, Dabble is the perfect app for all your DIYing needs. It transforms your Smartphone into a virtual I/O device and lets you control hardware via Bluetooth, communicate with it, access sensors like accelerometer, GPS, and proximity. It also provides you with dedicated projects compatible with Scratch and Arduino to help you learn by doing.
